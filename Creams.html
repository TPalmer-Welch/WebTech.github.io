Creams IceStone Gelato

Instagrammability - 6/10

Your order from Creams isn't photogenic. It feels as if it should be but it lacks pizzazz (yes that is a word, Google it). I'm not a photographer so I can't provide helpful tips, hopefully they do find one who will.

Make it Rain - 6/10

"It's not the cheapest here, is it?", is never a great thing to here halfway through your Cookie Dough. It's greatness increases somewhat if you can answer with "I know, but you can see why". This got 6 out of 10.

Cookie Dough - 5/10

Creams and Cookie Dough are not a tasty combination. It's clear they've gone for bigger is better. The problem here is that when a Cookie Dough starts to rise as much as the ones here do, you end with a cake. This could be salvaged (to an extent) if there was a generous amount of gooey-ness from the chocolate chips, it wasn't.

Background Information

I was today years old when I found out that there are a fair amount of Creams restaurants around the UK. As you'll see, this is quite surprising based off my Peterborough experience. Click me to arrive at their website. Making sure that link works was the first time I found out they do Bubble Pop Waffles, a lot of learning was done today.

Shakes - 5/10

As a naturally positive person, I'm (figuratively) a glass half full kinda guy. However, Creams managed to turn this saying into a reality as whatever I ordered definitely didn't pass the 50% mark - a very rare occasion.

Feng Shui - 8/10

Creams have seemed to take their notebooks when trying out Kaspa's. A very similar colour scheme and layout with comfortable seats make for an enjoyable sitting experience. Also all the walls on the toilet walls were mirrors. I couldn't find of (or think of) any mirror jokes. I'll put this in the self-reflection part of the report.
