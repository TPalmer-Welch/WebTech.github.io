Cookie Dough

Some might define this category as 'Biscuit Mix', but no one really calls Cookie Dough 'Biscuit Mix'. When judging a Dessert Cafe, only 1 dish is worth focusing any attention on and that is the almighty Cookie Dough. It's hard to describe the perfect cookie dough, its a heavenly balance of gooey-ness, thick-ness, sweet-ness, chocolately-ness and sticky-ness. If they master the best Cookie Dough, the other categories almost become meaningless.

Instagrammability

Some might define this category as 'Appearance' or 'Presentation', but those words exist in a dictionary and therefore too boring. A cafe's Instagrammability is how how likely you would be to upload your order to social media - to show off to the world the calories your about to consume. Not only does a particularly good looking dessert provide endless bragging rights but in the famous words of Richard Hammond, when garnishing Deep Fried Spaghetti Bolognese with Walkers Crisps, 'Sometimes, we eat with our eyes'. Well said.

Make it Rain

Some might define this category as 'Cost' or maybe even 'Value for Money', but again, at Desserted we take reviewing very seriously and do not associate ourselves with standard heading names. For a restaurant to score highly in this area, they must provide desserts that one enjoys so much that the price becomes irrelevant, or, when describing your experience with a friend and you mention the price, a look of intrigued (almost envious) shock develops on their face - not an easy feat.

Desserted

Desserted is website dedicated to reviewing Dessert Cafes. Currently, only 4 restaurants have been reviewed, but I needed 5 pages for this coursework so 4 will do. When reviewing a food based establishment, there must some criteria that each diner must be judged upon. Great experience with eating desserts have led to the formulation of 5 important categories (see below) that will be used as our marking scheme for each parlour's examination.

Shakes

Some might define this category as 'Milkshakes' or 'Sip it Slowly to Stop Brainfreeze', but in the extensive effort to be more efficient, 'Shakes' will do. This category also covers the Ice Creams on offer in each restaurant as they all offer milkshakes made with various scoops of their ice creams. Points are gained here for variety, thickness, taste and size, typically, the higher the better.

Feng Shui

Some might define this category as 'Internal Design' or 'Inside' but these aren't dramatic enough to be in a h2 tag. This category focuses on how nice each cafe is to sit and eat in, regardless of the quality of the food. Areas that are considered are the tables and chairs, bar area, walls, floors and toilet facilities. Ultimately - is it a nice place to be in.
